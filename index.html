About

Made To Wait Till Friday

━ Monthly Pantone Calendar is a 2021 color calendar for visual designers. This will inform designers what are the hottest colors to use for months + years. Everyone is welcome to use it for inspiration, color schemes, mood board, or has an obsession with pantones. I ranged the years from 2018 to 2022 since that is my limit to collect all the colors for the time being. If you select the year 2022, you’ll notice that all the colors are in black. That is because the upcoming color trends are unknown, so I wanted to create a mystery palette until each month has passed. For November + December 2021, I figured I should guess the color trends instead of coloring in black because I wanted the 2021 palette set to be complete.

━ There are currently 12 Pantone templates on the current site with a total of 61 colors in the palette. The numbers represented the holidays for the year 2022. For August + September, I left the days as 00 because I couldn’t find any holidays that people normally celebrate, so I left it as it is. When you first arrive at the page the current colors are chosen by my preference of how they are represented by weather + holidays. If you click on the year’s down arrow, you can change the years and see the colors change. I used Hirshfield + Sherwin Williams colors for reference. I could have used the Pantone Website, but it generalizes the color trend by year instead of months. Again, the overall colors are up to my preference, and should not solely rely upon these pantones for color trends accuracy.

Additional Elements

━ It wouldn’t be a calendar without a clock. Technically this is a countdown timer that does not have to do with the color schemes. The timer is used for when the school year ends. I thought the timer complements this site very glamorously.

━ This is the first time that I designed a cursor for my website. I thought I couldn’t do it, but I managed to succeed. I created the image on Canva and reduced the pixel size by 128 by 128. The half-round text reads, “MONTHLY PANTONE CALENDAR” which surrounds a brownish and pinkish blob.
